Prezentare generală:

    Taylor Alison Swift este o cantareata si scriitoare americana. Nascuta pe 16 decembrie 1989 în Pennsylvania, artista se muta la varsta de 14 ani în Tennesee pentru a-și urma visul de a deveni o cantareata de muzica country. În 2005 semnează un contract cu Big Machine Records urmand ca in 2006 sa fie scos pe piata primul sau album “Debut/Taylor Swift”.

   În 2005, Taylor Swift semnează un contract cu Big Machine Records urmand ca in 2006 sa fie scos pe piata primul sau album “Debut/Taylor Swift”. Acest prim album este urmat de inca 5, “Fearless” in 2008, “Speak Now” in 2010, “Red” in 2012, “1989” in 2014 si “Reputation” in 2016. Dupa ce a parasit Big Machine Records, Taylor Swift semnează cu Republic Records în 2018 și apoi lansează al șaptelea album al său “Lover”, in acelasi an. In 2020, în timpul lockdown-ului, scrie doua albume surori, “folklore” si “evermore”, ambele fiind nominalizate pentru Albumul anului la premiile Grammy. În octombrie 2020 Scooter Braun achizitioneaza drepturile pentru cele 6 albume pe care Taylor Swift le-a publicat cu Big Machine Records. Deși cântăreața și-a exprimat dorința de a putea sa își cumpere muzica nu i-a dat aceasta posibilitate așa ca a anunțat la puțin timp după ca are de gand sa înregistrează cele 6 albume.

   În prezent Taylor Swift este în procesul de a relansa primele 6 albume ale sale (Debut, Fearless, Speak Now, RED, 1989, Reputation), cu 2 dintre ele deja pe piața Fearless Taylor’s Version si R.E.D. Taylor’s Version.

   Muzica acesteia se întinde pe mai multe genuri, in principal country, pop si indie. Aceasta a jucat în mai multe filme (Cats(2020), Valentine's Day) și a terminat de curand filmările pentru "Amsterdam" regizat de David O. Russel. În prezent are 3 premii Grammy pentru Albumul anului (Fearless(2010), 1989(2016), folklore(2021)), fiind singura femeie din istorie care a realizat acest lucru. A regizat filmul Folklore: The Long Pond Studio Sessions(2020) și filmului scurt "All To Well: The Short Film"(2021), ce urmează sa fie prezentat la festivalul de film Tribeca.

   Pe 18 mai îi va fi acordat un doctorat onorariu de catre Universitatea din New York (unde în prezent exista un curs dedicat “evoluției lui Taylor Swift”) pentru arte frumoase. Aceasta onoare le-a fost acordată și altor artiști cum ar fi Ed Sheeran, Solange Knowles, Rihanna, Elton John, Celine Dion si multi altii.

“When Fearless did win Album of the Year at the Grammys and I did become the youngest solo artist to ever win the award, with that win came criticism and backlash in 2010 that I’d never experienced before as a young new artist. All of a sudden people had doubts about my singing voice, was it strong enough? Was I a little bit pitchy? All of a sudden they weren’t sure if I was the one writing the songs because sometimes in the past I had had co-writers in the room. [...]

So then I decided that I would be the only songwriter on my third album, Speak Now, and that I would tour constantly, work on my vocals every day, and perfect my stamina in a live show. I decided I would be what they said I couldn’t be. I didn’t know then that soon enough people would decide on something else I wasn’t quite doing right, and then the circle would keep going on and on and rolling along and I would keep accommodating, over-correcting, in an effort to appease my critics.

They’re saying I’m dating too much in my 20s? Okay, I’ll stop, I’ll just be single. For years. Now they’re saying my album Red is filled with too many breakup songs? Okay, okay, I’ll make one about moving to New York and deciding that really my life is more fun with just my friends. Oh, they’re saying my music is changing too much for me to stay in country music? All right. Okay, here’s an entire genre shift and a pop album called 1989.

Now it’s that I’m showing you too many pictures of me with my friends, okay, I can stop doing that too. Now I’m actually a calculated manipulator rather than a smart businesswoman? Okay, I’ll disappear from public view for years. Now I’m being cast a villain to you? Okay, here’s an album called Reputation and there are lots of snakes everywhere.

In the last 10 years I have watched as women in this industry are criticised and measured up to each other and picked at for their bodies, their romantic lives, their fashion, or have you ever heard someone say about a male artist, I really like his songs but I don’t know what it is, there’s just something about him I don’t like? No! That criticism is reserved for us!”

-Taylor Swift BMA’S 2019 dupa ce a castigat premiul “Woman of the Decade”
